Line&Scatter

This line and scatter chart shows the number of the Taiwanese tourists and the number of the foreign tourists between 2012 to 2022.

We can notice that Taiwanese people is really like to travel around the world. About 1/2 to 3/4 of Taiwanese people go traveling each year until 2020.

Additionally, we can see that the number of the Taiwanese tourists and foreign tourists increaseed year after year before 2020. In 2020 the number plummeted, and keeping going down in 2021 until 2022. Through this we can know that the epidemic serious impact the tourism in Taiwan.

Bar

This bar chart statistics the travel destinations of Taiwanese tourists each year. We can see that most of people choose to travel to Asia countries, followed by the American countries in order, and then European countries and Oceania and African countries. Additionally, we can see the number of tourists go down in 2022.

I guess the reason that make tourists like to travel to Asia more is "MONEY" and "FAMILY". First, if people want to travel to America or Europe, it cost really lots of money than Asia. Second, lots of people who are living in Taiwan actually from various Asia countries. Most of their families are living in the country of residence.
